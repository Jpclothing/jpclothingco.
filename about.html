About JP Clothing Co.

JP Clothing Co. is a minimalist menswear label founded to create clean, versatile pieces that fit modern life. From tailored essentials to the JP Active line for movement, we focus on quality materials and simple design.

JP Active

JP Active is our athletic sub-label — bold, performance-minded pieces designed with clean lines and a modern silhouette. The Active mark keeps visual ties to the main brand while introducing motion and energy.
